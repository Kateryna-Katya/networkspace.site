Triple Chili draws inspiration from Mayan and Aztec mythology — cultures that understood the real power behind their symbols and ceremonies.The visual language of the game is shaped by elements brought from the New World: peppers wrapped in flame, corn, fruits, and stone carvings that somehow feel both ancient and alive.

At the center of this world stands the pyramid — a symbol of balance and dominance.
It acts as a force that adapts, fills gaps, and reshapes outcomes when it appears.
What we ended up with? A game that actually feels substantial. It's got this warm, almost hypnotic quality that keeps pulling you back in.
